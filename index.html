Your dedication. Our promise.

We are convinced that every single person can have an impact. Because it starts with the small things.

We know that giving can be a joy. So we asked ourselves: Why is there still a burden to help other people? We believe in easy, obstacle-free solutions. Because sharing is caring. Our benefits:

Helping becomes tangible

Connect the act of donation into everyday routines. For example when drinking a bottle of soda you just bought.

Helping becomes transparent

Wouldn't you be happy, if you knew what impact you actually made? Because everyone can have an impact, we believe in transparency throught the whole process.

Helping becomes fun

Our easy to use platform allows for quick and convenient donations. Want to keep track of you donation history? Simply create an account!
